About Us

Technical and Business Management Institute. Now it is open as 1st year and 2nd year.

Vision: To provide quality technical and business education fostering innovation and leadership.

Mission: To empower students with skills and knowledge to excel in modern business and technology fields.

History: Established in 2004 in Panchagarh, TBMI has grown to offer premier programs in Humanities and Digital Technology.

Achievements: Recognized as one of the leading institutes in the region with high academic excellence and student achievements.
